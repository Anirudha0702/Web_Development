Christmas

Christmas is the most important festival among Christians. It is celebrated to mark the birth of Jesus, who is the supreme lord of the Christians. It marks the beginning of the days of hope for those oppressed before the birth of Jesus. Christmas celebrates the spirit of joy and merry-making.

Durga Pooja

Durga Pooja is a Hindu festival celebration of the Mother Goddess and the victory of the warrior Goddess Durga over the demon Mahisasura. The festival represents female power as ‘Shakti’ in the Universe. It is a festival of Good over Evil. Durga Pooja is one of the greatest festivals of India.
